It’s a 2-month hybrid internship program by 3Skill, where you’ll spend 1 month learning through guided training and 1 month working on real-world projects with mentor support. The goal is to make you industry-ready with practical exposure.

Any student, fresher, or beginner passionate about learning and working on real projects can join. No prior experience required — mentors will guide you step-by-step.

Currently available domains:
• Web Development
• Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI/ML)
• Data Analytics
• Frontend Development

New domains are added every month based on student demand.

Month 2: Work on industry-level projects and build your portfolio. You’ll also receive weekly reviews and improvement feedback from mentors.
