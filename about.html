ABOUT 3SKILL

"WHERE DIVERSITY IN SKILLS MEETS DEDICATION"

3Skill is not just a startup — it’s a movement. A revolution in the Indian ed-tech space, born with a clear and urgent mission: to empower students and young professionals with real-world skills across multiple high-demand domains.

Founded by Satyajit Swain, a Full Stack Developer and IIT Patna student, and currently led by him as the Founder & CEO, 3Skill stands as a symbol of transformation — from traditional bookish education to a more skill-driven, project-based, and industry-aligned learning ecosystem. From humble beginnings to becoming a multi-disciplinary ed-tech powerhouse, 3Skill today has a core team of 40+ professionals, trainers, developers, designers, marketers, data scientists, and industry experts.

Since 2024, 3Skill has trained over 1500+ students through hands-on live projects, real-time mentorship, and expert guidance — empowering every learner to become industry-ready, regardless of background or experience.

OUR CORE DOMAINS OF EXPERTISE

While we began with website development, our vision has always been bigger. Today, we operate in over 10+ core domains:

Full Stack Development (HTML, CSS, JS, React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB), AI & Machine Learning, Data Science, Social Media Management & Growth Marketing, UI/UX Design, Content Creation (Video Editing, Writing, Branding), Cybersecurity, Cloud & DevOps, App Development (Flutter, Android), Freelancing & Business Development.

Our teams are structured for real-world collaboration — students not only learn from us but often work with us on live projects, startups, and campaigns.

WHAT MAKES 3SKILL DIFFERENT?

  • Live Projects: Every student gets to work on real assignments, not dummy projects.
  • Mentorship Driven: 1-on-1 mentorship, weekly reviews, and career guidance.
  • Tool-Based Training: GitHub, Canva, VS Code, Firebase, Meta Ads & 100+ tools covered hands-on.
  • Job-Focused: Resume building, mock interviews, freelance & startup exposure.
  • Remote + Offline Workshops: In IITs, NITs & rural colleges to bridge the skill divide.
  • Scalable Learning: From weekend classes to 2-month bootcamps — for all learners.

100% INTERNSHIP GUARANTEE COURSE

Our program equips students with job-ready skills via a hands-on curriculum. With guaranteed internships upon course completion, learners gain technical expertise and work experience. Covering Full Stack Dev, AI/ML, Data Science & Digital Marketing — students graduate ready for the workforce.

OUR BELIEF

We believe skills are the true currency of the modern world. Degrees matter, but skills open doors. At 3Skill, every student is not just a learner but a future builder, innovator, and leader. Whether you dream of a job at Google, launching your startup, or freelancing from your village — we’ll walk with you, mentor you, and skill you up for success.
